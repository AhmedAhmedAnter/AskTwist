Your sassy AI sidekick with attitude. An Arabic AI chatbot powered by Google Gemini, packed with sarcasm, creativity, and Egyptian humor.

Try It Now

How It Works

Sarcastic Personality

Twist is engineered with a unique personality that mimics Egyptian humor. It's witty, sometimes chaotic, and always entertaining.

Personalized Memory

Using Firebase, the AI remembers your name and past interactions, creating sarcastic "notes" to personalize your chat experience.

Built-in AI Tools

All tools use dedicated UIs and custom Gemini prompts for hilarious results.

Tool Name Description
🌙 Dream Interpreter Enter your dream and get a ridiculous interpretation.
🏃 Excuse Generator Need a reason to be late? Twist creates absurd excuses.
🤔 Decision Maker Can’t choose? Twist will decide for you (his way).
📜 AI Poet Request a short poem. Expect satire or nonsense rhymes.
😏 Compliment Generator Passive-aggressive praise that sounds nice... but isn’t.
✨ Horoscope Get your fake fortune-teller reading based on your star sign.

🍳 Recipe Generator Tell Twist an ingredient, and he'll invent a wild dish.

Tech Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, Tailwind CSS, Vanilla JS
  • Backend: Firebase Firestore
  • Auth: Firebase Auth (Google Sign-in)
  • AI: Google Gemini 2.5 Flash
  • Audio: Tone.js
  • Canvas: HTML Canvas API for image cards
